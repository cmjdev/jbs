ABOUT US

Jones' Barbershop is a family owned barbershop serving the Greenwood community for years. Day in and day out Jones' has been serving up the simplest and freshest haircuts. Not only do we cut hair, but we also provide great barbershop conversations. Don't let this shy your kids away though, as we make sure to do our best to keep all conversations age appropriate around women and children. Let the record speak for itself, as we boast a 5 star rating on booksy, and facebook. Walk in or schedule today for a chance to get an amazing haircut from our crew.

Jones' Barber Shop is conveniently located in Greenwood, Indiana off of Smith Valley Road.
